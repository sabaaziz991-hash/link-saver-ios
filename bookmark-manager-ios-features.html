Feature Set at a Glance

External destination: linkbooked.com

Share Sheet Capture

Pin LinkBook to Share Sheet favorites so URLs can be saved from Safari and other apps in one tap.

Tags, Folders, and Filters

Use tags and folders to keep a large library manageable, then filter by favorites, unread, source, and more.

Reader + Offline Copy

Reader actions include saving offline copies and reopening article content with fewer distractions.

Smart Cards

Report and app text highlight domain-aware cards for platforms like TikTok, Spotify, GitHub, and Reddit.

Private iCloud Sync

Sync is presented as a premium, privacy-oriented option for your own Apple devices.

PDF and EPUB Import

Import files into the app so your reading workflow can include saved links and local documents.
