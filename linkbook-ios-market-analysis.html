Key Findings

Summarized from report pages 1-5 and 10-12.

Positioning

The report frames LinkBook as a native-first Apple productivity app rather than a generic cross-platform tool.

Privacy Model

It repeatedly highlights local storage, low data collection posture, and an anti-surveillance stance.

Media Adaptation

Smart Cards are described as a response to social-first information sources like TikTok, X, Spotify, and Reddit.
